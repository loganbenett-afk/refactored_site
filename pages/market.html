Why Hospitality Real Estate?

Unlike traditional multifamily or office assets, hotels offer daily pricing elasticity. This means that in an inflationary environment, room rates can be adjusted immediately to offset rising costs and capture increased demand, making hospitality one of the most effective inflation-hedged asset classes.

By focusing on select-service and extended-stay properties within major brand families (Marriott, Hilton, IHG), we benefit from lean operating models and high margins.

The Sunbelt Strategy

Decades Capital Group heavily focuses on the Sunbelt region, particularly states like Texas, Florida, and Georgia. These areas are experiencing unprecedented population growth and corporate relocations, driving both corporate travel and leisure demand.

Favorable tax environments, business-friendly policies, and high quality of life continue to attract major employers, creating a steady, year-round demand for premium hospitality accommodations.
