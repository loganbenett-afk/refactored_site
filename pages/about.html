Why Invest in Hotels?

Long-Term Value

Hotels appreciate in value while producing consistent income—offering both growth and cash flow.

Consistent Daily Revenue

Unlike many real estate classes, hotels generate income every single day through multiple revenue channels.

Tax Efficiency

Hospitality investments can offer substantial tax benefits including depreciation, bonus depreciation, and deductible expenses.

Portfolio Diversification

Hotels respond to different economic cycles than traditional stocks and bonds, reducing overall portfolio risk.

Multiple Revenue Drivers

Room sales, food & beverage, meetings, events, and retail all contribute to resilient income streams.

Dynamic Market Flexibility

Room pricing can be adjusted daily, allowing hotels to maximize earnings during peak demand.

Inflation Protection

Hotel rates naturally rise alongside inflation, helping preserve the real value of invested capital.

Travel Industry Growth

As global travel continues to expand post-pandemic, hospitality assets are positioned for exceptional long-term demand.

Our Investment Models

Long-Term Growth Strategy

Participate in ground-up hotel development in high-opportunity markets.

Key Benefits:
  • Strong equity upside from market appreciation and value creation
  • Investor-aligned structures with no hidden waterfalls
  • Targeted IRRs of 20–30%
  • Significant tax advantages through depreciation and cost segregation
  • Typical hold period: 3–5 years
Best For: Investors focused on wealth building and tax efficiency.

Immediate Income Strategy

Acquire stabilized hotels with strong brand backing and operational history.

Key Benefits:
  • 10–12% preferred annual returns
  • Monthly direct deposits (marketed as quarterly for simplicity)
  • No development or stabilization risks
  • Low leverage, strong cash-flowing properties
  • Typical hold period: 2–3 years
Best For: Investors seeking predictable monthly income and capital protection.
